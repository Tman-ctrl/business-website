Our Story

Built on the Belief That
Altitude Shouldn’t Be a Barrier.

Dash Drones (Pty) Ltd, established by Thapelo Rapetswa, who saw an industry overdue for disruption. Manual window cleaning is slow, dangerous, and expensive, we changed all three. Dash Drones (Pty) Ltd is revolutionising facade maintenance in South Africa, making drone-powered window cleaning the new industry standard. Safer for workers, better for clients, and kinder to the environment.

What is Dash Drones

Dash Drones is an extraordinary technology startup set to revolutionise window cleaning across South Africa. By leveraging autonomous drone technology, we solve the critical pain points of having to lift people 50 metres in the air to clean windows and eliminating slow cleaning times and high costs for businesses. Our purpose is to provide a quick clean at a lower cost, with a strong emphasis on sustainability and innovation. We are reducing the dangers of lifting people into the air.

The Reason Behind Dash Drones

The reason for starting Dash Drones was observing that people being lifted 50 metres in the air is dangerous, slow, and disruptive and can be extremely expensive. Property managers were paying too much for facade and window cleaning that took days even weeks, closed sidewalks, and risked lives. We replaced ropes with tethered drones.

Vision

Our vision is to build a drone services company that outlasts its founder and transforms how South Africa maintains its buildings, creates skilled jobs in drone technology, and proves that innovation and safety can go hand in hand.

Mission

Our mission is to revolutionise facade maintenance in South Africa by making drone-powered window cleaning the new industry standard and making it safer for workers, better for clients, and kinder to the environment.

We are building a multi-year growth roadmap starting with commercial high-rises in Gauteng, primarily Pretoria, Sandton, and Johannesburg’s CBDs and university campuses including Tshwane University of Technology, and premium residential estates and markets that demand quality and consistency.

Main Core Values

Safety

We exist to remove humans from one of the most dangerous jobs in the world. Every decision we make from how we fly and how we assess a job site is governed by an unwavering commitment to safety. No contract is worth a life, and no deadline is worth a risk.

Precision

Window cleaning is a craft. Our drones follow exact flight paths, apply consistent pressure, and leave no streaks, no missed panels, and no damage. We do not cut corners at altitude or at ground level. The quality of our work is visible from the street.

Innovation

We chose drones because they are better, faster, safer, and more consistent than any rope team. We stay ahead by continuously improving our technology, processes, and people. When the industry moves, we move first.

Reliability

Commercial building owners and facilities managers sign long-term contracts with companies they trust. We show up on time, we do the job we promised, and we leave the site better than we found it. Our word is our most valuable asset.

Sustainability

Battery-powered drones produce zero emissions. We use eco-friendly, biodegradable cleaning products, minimise water waste through precision application, and operate in a way that building owners can proudly include in their ESG reports. Green is not a marketing angle, it is how we operate.
